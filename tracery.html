As more queer artists decide to create work in the gaming industry, having the ability to modify pronoun usage for players will be seen as a need. According to The Trevor Project "25% of LGBTQ youth use they/them exclusively, a combination of he/him, she/her, or they/them, or neopronouns such as ze/zir or fae/faer." To accomadate this growing percentage of the population, we should expand the options presented to players in regards to pronoun use. By expanding options, games bring awareness of underrepresented groups in the broader mediascape.
The Trevor Project. (2020, July 29). Pronoun Usage Among LGBTQ Youth. Retrieved October 9, 2025, from https://www.thetrevorproject.org/research-briefs/pronouns-usage-among-lgbtq-youth/
